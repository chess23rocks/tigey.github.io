Background

In the 1600's, European countries began establishing colonies in the "New World" of North America to establish trade routes, gain more resources, and expand their imperial rule. England was one of them. Over the 17th and 18th centuries, thirteen distinct English colonies were established on the east coast of North America in the present-day United States.
